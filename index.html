Background Story

The Shadow Shinobi In a small village hidden deep in the mountains, the nights were haunted by savage orcs that craved chaos. But the villagers slept soundly, knowing their protector lurked in the darkness. He was the Shadow Shinobi, a master of stealth and shadow magic, sworn to defend his people. Silent as the wind, he patrolled the village's borders each night, his blade striking faster than the eye could follow. Cloaked in darkness, he became one with the shadows, appearing only to strike down those who dared to threaten his home. One fateful night, a powerful orc shaman arrived, summoning an army of beasts. The Shinobi faced his greatest challenge yet. Without fear or hesitation, he vanished into the night, his blade ready, his mission clear: protect the village at all costs. And so, the legend of the Shadow Shinobi lived on, whispered in gratitude by those he saved.
