Background Story

Hidden deep within the mountains lay a quiet village, threatened each night by savage orcs hungry for chaos. Yet the villagers slept without fear — because someone watched over them: the Shadow Shinobi.

Silent as the wind and one with the darkness, he patrolled the borders every night. Those who dared to approach saw only a flash of steel… and then nothing.

But one night, a powerful orc shaman appeared, summoning an entire horde. It became the Shinobi’s greatest challenge.
Without hesitation, he vanished into the shadows, his blade ready, determined to protect his home at all costs.

Since that day, the villagers whisper of the warrior who fights unseen — their guardian in the dark.
